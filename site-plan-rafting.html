Overview

Purpose

The purpose of Dry Oar White Water Rafting website is to give rafting enthusiasts a great place to start their white water rafting adventure. We are a good informational site for seekers to find answers to questions about rafting - such as, what equipment should be taken when running a river? When is the best time and season to launch for the biggest rapids, or the calmest, most scenic, floats? How safe is river running? Any, and all, serious questions concerning white water rafting are encouraged. Afterall, the only bad questions are the one's that aren't asked. Dry Oar Rafting offers many adventure packages, everything from short three hour rapid runs, to six night-seven day excursions. The Alaska's Tana river, where we are located, offers all types of river experiences from class IV / V rapids for the extreme thrill seekers and experienced water hounds, to slow, wide, meandering sections, through some of the most breathtaking and awe-inspiring vistas imaginable, for those who desire a more relaxing experience. Whatever your desires Dry Oar is here to help you plan your amazing trip with confidence.

Audience

My site is friendly for all customers of all ages. No matter how much, or little, experience people have with river running there is something that will fit any need. We are ADA friendly as well. This site is for all adventurers looking for fun that is different and out of the ordinary. Individuals, couples, families, church groups, even larger corporate groups looking for an unforgetable experience on the river are welcome here. The only thing we ask is that everyone in healthy and can pass a health screening. Any young people under the age of 12 must be accompanied by a responsible adult. and all young people from 13 to 18 must have a consent form signed and notorized before launch.
